Delivery Order Policy

Need to cancel or modify your delivery? Here's our policy:

  • Partial refunds (75%): Cancellations between 1-2 hours before delivery.
  • Late cancellations: No refunds if canceled less than 1 hour before delivery.

Delivery Issues

If there's a problem with your delivery:

  1. Late deliveries: If your order arrives more than 30 minutes past the estimated delivery time, contact us for a partial refund of delivery fees.
  2. Quality issues: Check your order immediately upon delivery. For temperature issues, incorrect items, or damaged products, take photos and contact us within 30 minutes of delivery.
  3. Missing items: Report any missing items within 1 hour of delivery with your order number.

Delivery-Specific Refunds

  • Delivery fee refunds: Full refund of delivery fees for late deliveries (30+ minutes past estimated time).
  • Undelivered orders: Full refund including delivery fees if we're unable to complete your delivery.
  • Wrong address: No refund if the delivery address you provided was incorrect.

Delivery FAQs

Q: What if I'm not available at delivery time?
A: Our driver will wait for 5 minutes and attempt to contact you. After that, they'll leave the order in a safe place if possible and send a photo notification. No refunds for missed deliveries.

Q: Can I change my delivery address after ordering?
A: Address changes must be requested at least 1 hour before delivery and may incur additional fees if outside our delivery zone.

Q: How do I report issues with my delivery?
A: Email lotusbrewcafeph@gmail.com with your order number and photos (if applicable) within 1 hour of delivery.

How Refunds Are Processed

  • Credit/debit card refunds: 1-2 business days.
  • Gift cards: Credited back within 24 hours.
